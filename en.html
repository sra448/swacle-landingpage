What is Swacle?

Swacle is an open swap circle for Funko Pop collectors.

Why use Swacle?

Swacle offers each registered user an individualized and saveable search with notification when a hit is found. In addition, you get information and contacts for the Funko Pop community.

How long does a swap take?

Each of the three phases can take different amounts of time. It is not possible to predict an exact schedule for a swap.

Who are we?

We are two nerds from Switzerland. One nerd is a programmer, the other is a student and Funko Pop, Anime and Manga fan. We do this in our free time for fun.

Can all kinds of Funko Pops be exchanged at Swacle?

Yes, all Funko Pop vinyl figures
